Date Methods

The internal clock in JavaScript starts at midnight January 1, 1970 (zero point).

The methods of the Date object will be examined here.

new Date() object:
getTime() (the milliseconds since zero point)
getDay() (0 to 6)
Short day from array
All short days from array
Full day from array and string
getDate() (1 to 31)
getMonth() (0 to 11)
getFullYear() (yyyy)
getHours() (0 to 23)
getMinutes() (0 to 59)
getSeconds() (0 to 59)
getMilliseconds() (0 to 999)
